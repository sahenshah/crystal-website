About Us

At Crystal Industries LTD, we take pride in being part of everyday life in East Africa by crafting high-quality aluminum cooking pots that are trusted by families across the region. We understand how important reliable cookware is in every kitchen, and that's why we put care into every piece we make.

Visit Us Today

We're proud to employ around 150 people, all from the local community, on both permanent and temporary terms. Our team is at the heart of what we do, and their dedication helps us not only grow as a company, but also support local livelihoods and reduce unemployment in meaningful ways.
