About Us

At Crystal Industries, we are proud producers of durable and affordable aluminum cookware, offering a wide range of cooking pots, kettles, and lids. As a trusted name under the Crystal brand, we combine precision manufacturing with quality craftsmanship, delivering products that meet the needs of both everyday homes and professional kitchens. We specialize in the production of aluminum cooking pots in a variety of gauges — from lightweight to extra-extra heavy. We use recyclable aluminium as one of our primary raw materials. This not only reduces environmental impact but also supports a circular economy by giving new life to valuable materials. Our products are made with care — for your kitchen and for the planet. Every sufuria and kettle is proudly made in Kenya — not just assembled, but fully crafted. The entire process, from melting raw aluminium to pressing and finishing, is done locally at our facility. Kenya remains the main market for our products, Crystal's well-known brands are well established in Ethiopia, Malawi, Rwanda, South Sudan, Tanzania and Uganda.
